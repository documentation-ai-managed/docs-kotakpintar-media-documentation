## Upload Methods

Choose the method that best fits your workflow.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Interface" icon="globe">
    Use our drag-and-drop interface for quick uploads.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Upload" icon="upload">
        Click the upload button in your dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Files" icon="file">
        Drag files into the upload area or click to browse.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Metadata" icon="tag">
        Enter titles, descriptions, and tags for organization.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Process Upload" icon="play">
        Click upload to process your files.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Callout kind="tip">Bulk uploads support up to 100 files simultaneously.</Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Upload" icon="code">
    Integrate uploads directly into your applications.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
      ````javascript
      const formData = new FormData();
      formData.append('file', fileInput.files[0]);
      formData.append('title', 'My Media File');
      formData.append('tags', 'important,project');
      
      fetch('/api/media/upload', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: {
          'Authorization': `Bearer ${token}`
        },
        body: formData
      })
      .then(response => response.json())
      .then(data => console.log('Upload successful:', data));
      ````
      ````python
      import requests
      
      files = {'file': open('media.mp4', 'rb')}
      data = {'title': 'Sample Video', 'tags': 'demo,video'}
      headers = {'Authorization': f'Bearer {token}'}
      
      response = requests.post('https://api.kotakpintar.media/media/upload', 
                             files=files, data=data, headers=headers)
      print('Upload response:', response.json())
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

Follow these guidelines for optimal upload performance.

- Compress large files before uploading to reduce transfer time.
- Use descriptive filenames and metadata for better organization.
- Verify file integrity after upload using checksums.
- Schedule bulk uploads during off-peak hours to avoid congestion.
